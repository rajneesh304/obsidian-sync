Terminology

1. Broker: Server(physical or logical) that holds a queue
2. Partition: The queue. An ordered, immutable sequence of messages
that we append to, like a log file
A broker can have multiple partitions
3. Topic: A logical grouping of partitions. We publish to and consume 
from a topic 
4. Producers/Consumers: Writes/Reads messages to/from a topic ^gtKpuLiE
